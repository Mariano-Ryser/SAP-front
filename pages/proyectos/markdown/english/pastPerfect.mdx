


# Small sentences in English to refresh my head.!

## Past perfect

She had already finished her homework before her friends arrived.

By the time I got to the cinema, the movie had already started.

They had eaten dinner before the guests arrived at their house.

He had studied English for several years before he moved to the United States.

By the time she woke up, her family had already left for the vacation.

We had already booked our tickets before the concert was announced.

The chef had prepared all the ingredients before the cooking class began.

They had lived in that neighborhood for ten years before they decided to move.

By the time the train arrived, I had already missed it.

She had read the book before she watched the movie adaptation.
